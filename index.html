Accessibile Design

For this assignment, I focused on what aspects of design can be used to help with accessibility, specifically for those with low vision.

Course Goals


1. Consider the role of rhetoric, technology, and ethics in acts of making.

Design need to last years and uphold it's purpose. People need to respond to design in a positive way, and the only way to do that is through analysing their rections. Design needs to be accessibile to have a positive response, which is why we focused on Accessible design and I chose to research design elements for people who may have low vision.

Design isn't just meant to be seen on billboards and flyers, but it utilizes technology in everyway. Basically everything we hold today has some sort of design aspect, and it's important that we make it ethical.

2. Leverage Design Principles.

For all of the designs I created, I tried to pick a motif and stick with it. For example, in the Gothic Architecture Infographic, I chose the motif of buildings with green and purple hues. For my Advertisement I chose the repetition of white rectangles- meant to look like a cross walk- and for my Designing for Low Vision assignemnt I chose yellow bubbles around the numbers. Repetition and having a motif were just two of the features that we learned in our eatly discussions of good design principles.

3. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

Being able to talk to others in class about different design aspects has significantly helped my design process. I am able to speak about different design features and workshop my designs in class. When something is mentioned, I try my best to change it while we are on the topic of discussion.



4. Practice revision within and across media forms.

What was nice about completing all of these projects was that we were activley able to recieve feedback from our peers during class. They were able to critic my work and give me ways that I could improve my design.

5. Learn to present your work.

For many of these projects I have been given the opportunity to present my work. I like being able to practive my public speaking skills. When we talk in groups in class, it also helps me to work on my communcation skills. I enjoy hearing other people's opinions when it comes to design, and it has prepared me to recieve feedback.
